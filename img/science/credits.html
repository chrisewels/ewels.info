Credits

The images shown here were created using povray, the ray tracing package.

Many thanks to:

  • Some molecular structures were taken from the work of Phillipe Lambin on kinked nanotubes and Roger Taylor and Adam Dawlish on fluorinated fullerenes.
  • Galaxy effect used with C60 comes from Chris Colefax .
  • The flowers used in the silicon picture came from Gordon.
  • The excellent earth came from Constantine Thomas' Planetary Map Site, constructed by Constantine from a map by James Hastings-Trew using USGS data.
Just for the record, all structures were taken from genuine molecular calculations and are either structurally optimised with AIMPRO (a density functional code) or tight binding.
